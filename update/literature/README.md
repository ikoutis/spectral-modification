# Literature Cache

This directory stores open-access PDFs used by `../spectral-maximizer.tex`.

## Downloaded PDFs

- `peng-approx-undirected-max-flow-hierarchical-decompositions-2015.pdf`:
  Richard Peng, approximate undirected max flow and hierarchical cut decompositions.
- `henzinger-munk-racke-hierarchical-congestion-approximator-2025.pdf`:
  Henzinger, Munk, and Racke, improved near-linear hierarchical congestion approximators.
- `li-rao-wang-bottom-up-congestion-approximators-2025.pdf`:
  Li, Rao, and Wang, bottom-up congestion approximators.
- `lee-peng-spielman-sparsified-cholesky-schur-sparsifiers-2015.pdf`:
  Lee, Peng, and Spielman, sparsified Cholesky and Schur-complement sparsifiers.
- `li-schild-spectral-subspace-schur-sparsification-2018.pdf`:
  Li and Schild, spectral subspace sparsification and Schur complement sparsifiers.
- `black-wan-nayyeri-wang-effective-resistance-oversquashing-2023.pdf`:
  Black, Wan, Nayyeri, and Yusu Wang, effective resistance rewiring for oversquashing.
- `karhadkar-banerjee-montufar-fosr-2023.pdf`:
  FoSR spectral rewiring for GNN oversquashing.
- `attali-pernelle-buscaldi-malliaros-gnn-rewiring-survey-2026.pdf`:
  2026 survey of GNN rewiring methods.
- `hanauer-henzinger-munk-racke-votsch-expander-hierarchies-ncut-2026.pdf`:
  Expander hierarchies for normalized cut clustering.
